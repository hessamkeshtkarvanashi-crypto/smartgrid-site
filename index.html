About Our Group

Our research group focuses on Smart Grids, Microgrid Systems, and Cyber-Physical Energy Infrastructure. We explore intelligent energy management, power optimization, and secure communication between distributed systems.

About Faculty Advisor

Our research focuses on Power Systems, Smart Grids, Microgrid Systems, and Cyber-Security.
